Career Goals
I hope to obtain a full-time job involving the application of software design and research, use of coding skills in any language, prferrably C/C#, and strong group collaboration and team communication.

I am finishing my final year of college at The Ohio State University where I am studying Computer Science and Engineering with specialization in AI technology. I hope to be graduating in early May of 2023. So I hope to be able to take on a full-time job as soon as I have gotten through commencement. The specifics I'm most looking for in a job, but not necessarily need, are:
  1. The chance to use my skill in programming, such as coding in C/C#, Java, Python, etc.
  2. Opportunities to work on challenging tasks that will lead to personal, group, and especially company goals
  3. The chance to work with other people to use my communication and collaboration skills I have built up throughout college and my work experiences
In my work, I enjoy a challenge, and am always willing to take on anything no matter the difficulty of the task. For me, it is most important that work or a project is finished, and finished with effort and care. Because of this, once I start work on an assigned project, I put my time and effort into making sure the job is well done and thoroughly complete.

My goal when working for my company and team is to be a model employee and coworker, open to all types of ideas and work, and provide help and encouragement to all my team members and other coworkers. I want to be a positive and helpful member of the company and work to improve the community and ethics of the business. I especially want to put my best foot forward, and work my hardest to produce the best possible work. And outside of work, I want to be a leading example of what our company means and provides for the public.
